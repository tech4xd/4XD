Frequently Ask Questions

How much does it cost to build a website?

Pricing starts at £997 for a custom starter site. The final cost depends on your design, features, and business needs. We’ll provide a tailored quote after understanding your goals.

Do you provide a website hosting service?

Yes, we offer reliable hosting as an optional add-on for an additional cost. You’re also welcome to use your own hosting provider, and we’ll guide you through either setup to ensure everything runs smoothly.

Will I be able to update the website?

Discuss your goals with our team, and we’ll recommend the best approach—whether that means managing updates on your own or having us handle them for you.

Is SEO included in my website project?

Yes, all packages include basic SEO setup. We can also discuss enhanced SEO services tailored to your industry and growth goals.
